Mars

At Mars we take our responsibility for marketing our brands appropriately very seriously. We have a Marketing Code that governs all our promotional activity and states that we only promote our products to people aged 13 and over as this is the age at which we believe that people can make informed choices about sensible snack consumption. We apply our Marketing Code to all our advertising and communications and are committed to providing you and your family with suitable and transparent information about our products.
